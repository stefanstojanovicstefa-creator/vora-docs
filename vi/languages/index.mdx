# Ho tro ngon ngu

Vora ho tro hon 100 ngon ngu qua 12 nha cung cap ASR, 11 nha cung cap TTS va 15 nha cung cap LLM.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Don gian">
    ## Ngon ngu duoc ho tro

    Vora ho tro tat ca cac ngon ngu chinh tren the gioi. Day la **17 ngon ngu uu tien** cua chung toi:

    | Ngon ngu | Khu vuc | Cuoc goi | Tro chuyen |
    |----------|---------|:--------:|:----------:|
    | Tieng Viet | Viet Nam | Co | Co |
    | Indonesia | Indonesia | Co | Co |
    | Malay | Malaysia, Singapore | Co | Co |
    | Tagalog | Philippines | Co | Co |
    | Thai | Thai Lan | Co | Co |
    | A-rap | Trung Dong | Co | Co |
    | Hindi | An Do | Co | Co |

    Cong them **hon 80 ngon ngu khac**.

    ### Cach dat ngon ngu cho agent

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Mo agent cua ban">
        Di den Agent va chon agent cua ban.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Chon ngon ngu">
        Trong cai dat giong noi, chon ngon ngu tu danh sach.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Luu">
        Nhan Luu. Agent cua ban bay gio hieu va noi bang ngon ngu da chon.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Nang cao">
    ## Ma tran pham vi nha cung cap

    Vora tich hop voi **12 ASR**, **11 TTS** va **15 LLM** nha cung cap tren hon 100 ngon ngu.

    De so sanh chi tiet:
    - [Nha cung cap ASR](/languages/asr-providers) - So sanh 12 nha cung cap giong noi thanh van ban
    - [Nha cung cap TTS](/languages/tts-providers) - So sanh 11 nha cung cap van ban thanh giong noi
    - [Nha cung cap LLM](/languages/llm-providers) - So sanh 15 nha cung cap mo hinh ngon ngu

    ### Cau hinh

    Dat ngon ngu cho tung agent trong Trung tam Chi huy:
    - **Tab Tai** - Ngon ngu STT de nhan dang giong noi
    - **Tab Giong noi** - Ngon ngu TTS de xuat am thanh
    - **Tab Nao** - Ngu canh ngon ngu LLM de tao phan hoi
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
