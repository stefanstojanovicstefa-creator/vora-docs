    ### How to Set Your Agent's Language

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your agent">
        Go to Agents and select your agent.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose language">
        In the voice settings, select the language from the dropdown.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Click Save. Your agent now understands and speaks in the selected language.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Most agents work best with a single primary language. For multilingual support, contact us about our multi-language agent configuration.

    - **Voice Tab** - TTS language for speech output
