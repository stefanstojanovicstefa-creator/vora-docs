## Tai sao chon Vora?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Thiet ke uu tien giong noi">
    Xay dung tu dau cho hoi thoai giong noi thoi gian thuc. Do tre duoi 500ms, chuyen doi luot noi tu nhien va ho tro ngat loi qua LiveKit WebRTC.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hon 100 ngon ngu duoc ho tro">
    Tu tieng A-rap va Hindi den tieng Viet va Thai. Vora ho tro hon 100 ngon ngu qua 12 nha cung cap ASR, 11 nha cung cap TTS va 15 nha cung cap LLM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Mang nha cung cap cua ban">
    Su dung Gemini, GPT-4, Claude hoac Groq cho LLM. Deepgram, Google hoac AssemblyAI cho STT. ElevenLabs, Cartesia hoac Google cho TTS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Che do Don gian va Nang cao">
    Nguoi dung doanh nghiep co trai nghiem don gian. Lap trinh vien mo khoa Trung tam Chi huy, chuc nang tuy chinh, giam sat va truy cap API.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Bat dau

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tao tai khoan">
    Dang ky tai [app.voicevora.com](https://app.voicevora.com) - goi mien phi co san khong can the tin dung.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tao agent dau tien">
    Su dung phuong phap **Phong van** - chi can mo ta nhung gi ban muon va Vora se tao cho ban.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Thu nghiem va trien khai">
    Thu nghiem trong trinh mo phong, sau do trien khai voi so dien thoai hoac widget web.
  </Step>
</Steps>
