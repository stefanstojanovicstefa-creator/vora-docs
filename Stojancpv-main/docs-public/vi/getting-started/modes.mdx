# Che do Don gian & Nang cao

Bang dieu khien va tai lieu cua Vora cung cap hai che do de ban luon thay muc do chi tiet phu hop.

---

## Cach hoat dong

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Don gian">
    **Che do Don gian** la trai nghiem mac dinh. Duoc thiet ke cho nguoi dung doanh nghiep muon tao va quan ly agent giong noi ma khong lo lang ve chi tiet ky thuat.

    Trong Che do Don gian, ban se thay:
    - Quy trinh tao agent don gian (phuong phap Phong van va Nhap URL)
    - Chi cac cai dat can thiet (giong noi, ngon ngu, loi chao)
    - Bang dieu khien sach se voi cac chi so chinh
    - Quy trinh lam viec co huong dan voi meo huu ich

    <Tip>
      Che do Don gian la hoan hao neu ban muon khoi dong agent nhanh chong. Ban luon co the chuyen sang Che do Nang cao sau.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Nang cao">
    **Che do Nang cao** mo khoa toan bo suc manh cua nen tang Vora. Duoc thiet ke cho lap trinh vien va nguoi dung chuyen nghiep can kiem soat chi tiet.

    Trong Che do Nang cao ban co tat ca trong Che do Don gian cong them:
    - **Trung tam Chi huy** - Cong cu tao agent 9 tab voi kiem soat nha cung cap day du
    - **Che do Prompt** - Chinh sua prompt he thong truc tiep
    - **Chuc nang Tuy chinh** - Tao va gan cac loi goi cong cu tuy chinh
    - **Giam sat** - Suc khoe he thong thoi gian thuc va cac chi so
    - **Khoa API** - Quan ly khoa API va mang khoa nha cung cap cua ban

    <Info>
      Che do Nang cao hien thi tat ca tuy chon cau hinh. Mot so tinh nang nhu Chuc nang Tuy chinh va Giam sat chi hien thi trong Che do Nang cao.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Chuyen doi che do

### Tren bang dieu khien

Nhan vao nut chuyen doi che do o goc tren ben phai thanh ben:
- **Don gian** (bieu tuong ngoi sao) - Giao dien don gian
- **Nang cao** (bieu tuong cai dat) - Giao dien lap trinh vien day du

Lua chon cua ban duoc tu dong luu va giu lai qua cac phien.
