# Bat dau nhanh

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Don gian">
    Khoi dong agent giong noi Vora dau tien trong 3 buoc de dang. Khong can lap trinh.

    ## Truoc khi bat dau

    Ban can:
    - Tai khoan Vora ([dang ky mien phi](https://app.voicevora.com/signup))
    - Micro de thu nghiem
    - Khoang 5 phut

    ---

    ## Buoc 1: Tao Agent

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Di den Agent">
        Tu bang dieu khien, nhan **Agent** o thanh ben, roi nhan **Tao Agent**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Chon phuong phap Phong van">
        Chon **Phong van** lam phuong phap tao. Vora se hoi ban ve agent va tao cho ban.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Chon giong noi va ngon ngu">
        Chon giong noi tu thu vien va chon ngon ngu cho agent. Vora ho tro hon 100 ngon ngu.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ---

    ## Buoc 2: Thu nghiem Agent

    Nhan nut **Thu nghiem** va bat dau noi chuyen. Thu mot vai kich ban.

    ---

    ## Buoc 3: Trien khai

    Nhan **Trien khai**, chon kenh (so dien thoai hoac widget web), va nhan **Kich hoat**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Nang cao">
    Khoi dong agent giong noi Vora dau tien voi kiem soat day du nha cung cap va cau hinh.

    ## Truoc khi bat dau

    Ban can:
    - Tai khoan Vora ([dang ky mien phi](https://app.voicevora.com/signup))
    - Micro de thu nghiem
    - Khoang 5 phut
    - (Tuy chon) Khoa API nha cung cap cua ban

    ---

    ## Buoc 1: Tao Agent

    Chon phuong phap tao: Phong van (khuyen nghi), Nhap URL, Che do Prompt, hoac Trung tam Chi huy.

    ## Buoc 2: Cau hinh nha cung cap

    | Thanh phan | Nha cung cap mac dinh | Thay the |
    |------------|----------------------|----------|
    | **STT** | Deepgram | Google, AssemblyAI, Soniox |
    | **LLM** | Gemini Flash | GPT-4o, Claude, Groq |
    | **TTS** | ElevenLabs | Cartesia, Google, Azure |

    ## Buoc 3: Thu nghiem va Trien khai

    Thu nghiem trong trinh mo phong, sau do trien khai qua so dien thoai, widget web hoac API.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
