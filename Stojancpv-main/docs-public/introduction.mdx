## Why Vora?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Voice-first by design">
    Built from the ground up for real-time voice conversations. Sub-500ms latency, natural turn-taking, and barge-in support via LiveKit WebRTC.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="100+ languages supported">
    From Arabic and Hindi to Vietnamese and Thai. Vora supports 100+ languages across 12 ASR providers, 11 TTS providers, and 15 LLM providers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bring your own providers">
    Use Gemini, GPT-4, Claude, or Groq for LLM. Deepgram, Google, or AssemblyAI for STT. ElevenLabs, Cartesia, or Google for TTS. Mix and match per agent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Simple and Advanced modes">
    Business users get a streamlined experience in Simple Mode. Developers unlock the full Command Center, custom functions, monitoring, and API access in Advanced Mode.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Knowledge Base built-in">
    Upload documents, crawl websites, or connect databases. Your agents understand your business context through RAG-powered retrieval.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deploy anywhere">
    Phone numbers, web embeds, WhatsApp, SIP trunks, or custom integrations. One agent, multiple channels.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [app.voicevora.com](https://app.voicevora.com) - free tier available with no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first agent">
    Use the **Interview** method - just describe what you want and Vora builds it for you. Or import from a URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and deploy">
    Test in the simulator, then deploy with a phone number or web widget.
  </Step>
</Steps>
